Managing Your Subscriptions

Keeping track of various subscriptions for online services can be challenging. It’s often difficult to get a clear overview of your monthly expenses. Subscription Manager is here to help you manage and monitor all your subscriptions in one place.

About Subscription Manager

Subscription Manager is a powerful tool designed to simplify the way you handle your subscriptions. Here are some of the key features:

  • Track all your subscriptions in one place
  • Get reminders for upcoming renewals
  • Analyze your monthly expenses
  • Easy-to-use interface

How to Use Subscription Manager

Adding Subscriptions
  • Navigate to the 'Add Subscription' section.
  • Fill in the details of your subscription.
  • Click the 'Save' button to add the subscription.

Viewing Subscriptions
  • Go to the 'My Subscriptions' section.
  • View all your subscriptions listed.
  • Sort and filter subscriptions as needed.

Editing Subscriptions
  • Click on a subscription in the 'My Subscriptions' section.
  • Edit the details of the subscription.
  • Click the 'Save' button to update the subscription.

Setting Reminders
  • Navigate to the 'Settings' section.
  • Set reminders for upcoming renewals.
  • Save your settings to avoid missing important dates.
