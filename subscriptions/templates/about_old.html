About Subscription Manager

Subscription Manager is a powerful tool designed to simplify the way you handle your subscriptions. Here are some of the key features:

  • Track all your subscriptions in one place
  • Get reminders for upcoming renewals, cancellation etc
  • Analyze your monthly expenses
  • Easy-to-use interface

How to Use Subscription Manager

Adding Subscriptions
  • Click the add icon at the top of the subscription list.
  • Choose a service from the list.
  • Click the 'Save' button to add the subscription.

Viewing Subscriptions
  • Choose Subscriptions from the menu.
  • Click on a subscription to see the details.

Editing Subscriptions
  • Click on a subscription in the list.
  • Click the Edit button
  • Click the 'Save' button to update the subscription when you're done editing.

Setting Reminders
  • Click Edit for the subscription you want to add a reminder for.
  • Enter an email address if you want to receive an e-mail.
  • Add reminder date and a message.
